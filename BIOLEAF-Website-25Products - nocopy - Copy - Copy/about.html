About BIOLEAF

BIOLEAF was established in 2025 with a clear vision to promote natural, eco-friendly, and biodegradable disposable tableware products. Based in Lucknow and Basti, Uttar Pradesh, we are dedicated to providing sustainable alternatives for everyday use without compromising on quality or convenience. Our range includes cups, plates, spoons, and other disposable tableware, all designed with the environment in mind.

At BIOLEAF, we pride ourselves on using the latest manufacturing techniques and quality materials to produce biodegradable products that meet the highest standards. With a skilled and committed team, we ensure that every product reflects our dedication to environmental sustainability and customer satisfaction.
